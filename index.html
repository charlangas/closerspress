Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure books are fully personalized and accurate?

Our dedicated research team dives deep into public information, customer interviews, and any materials you provide to capture authentic, precise details for each book.

Can we review the book content before printing?

Absolutely. We build in revision rounds throughout the process to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the story, design, and print quality before finalizing.

How long does the book production process take?

Once we've agreed on a strategy and custom story framework with you, each book takes about 2 business days to go from concept to full digital draft. From digital draft to delivery, we can typically produce high-quality printed books within 1 week. However, timelines can vary based on quantity, complexity, and your team's feedback rounds.

How personalized can the books get?

Beyond customizing the story and illustrations, we can personalize each book with the recipient's name and other specific details, making each copy uniquely tailored.

How do you ensure the books reach the right decision-makers?

We work closely with you to verify mailing addresses and conduct research to ensure each book is delivered directly to the hands of your intended high-value prospects.
