## Why Smart Founders Are Abandoning the Inbox.
If you send a cold email to a Fortune 500 VP, it takes them 3 seconds to delete it. If you send them a cheap piece of direct mail, it goes into the lobby trash can. 

But human psychology makes it incredibly difficult to throw away a beautifully bound, hardcover book—especially one that tells a story about *their* business. It doesn't go in the trash. It stays on the desk.
